    How do I schedule a session? 

    Simply contact us through our website or phone number to book your appointment. We'll work with you to find a convenient time for your session.

    What can I expect during a coaching/therapy session?

    Each session is tailored to your needs and goals. You can expect a supportive and non-judgmental environment where you can discuss your concerns and work towards positive change.

    How many sessions will I need?

    The number of sessions varies depending on your individual circumstances and goals. We'll work with you to develop a personalized plan that meets your needs and fits your schedule.

    Is my information confidential?

    Yes, confidentiality is of the utmost importance to us. Your information will be kept strictly confidential in accordance with ethical guidelines and legal requirements.
